Rodeo Orchestra Poster

2024

A poster advertising a hypothetical performance of Rodeo, a ballet by Aaron Copeland. Scans of woodblock type, with French Clarendon and Tuscan characters, are used for the title of the performance and the letter R that forms the structure for the design.
