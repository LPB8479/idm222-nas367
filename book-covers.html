Agatha Christie Book Covers

2024

Agatha Christie's Hercule Poirot series follows the titular detective as he solves murder mysteries around the world. The novels all have a very similar, "cookie cutter" plotline, represented by the basic shapes, and the textures are reminiscent of passport stamps, reflecting Poirot's worldly adventures.
